# Outreach Messages

## Warm contact

Hi [Name] — I’m building a free tool for Peruvian small businesses that analyzes a government tender and highlights the mandatory requirements, possible disqualifiers, deadlines, missing evidence, and whether the opportunity appears worth pursuing.

I’m testing the first version with a small number of businesses. Have you reviewed a public tender before, or would you be open to sharing one opportunity for me to analyze with you?

## Follow-up

Hi [Name] — following up because I’m still looking for a few businesses that can share a real tender and walk me through how they decide whether to apply. The test is free, and I’ll send you a cited checklist from the document. Would a 20-minute conversation this week work?

## Request for a referral

Thanks — even if your company does not apply directly, do you know an owner, commercial lead, or bid coordinator who reviews SEACE opportunities? An introduction would be very helpful.

## After the test

Thank you for testing TenderFit. Could you send me three quick answers?

1. Did the report identify anything you had missed?
2. Did it make the opportunity easier to evaluate?
3. Would you use it again on another tender?
