# TenderFit LATAM — MVP Product Spec

## Problem

Small businesses can spend hours reading procurement documents before discovering a mandatory condition that makes them ineligible. TenderFit reduces that initial screening effort while keeping the official document as the source of truth.

## Primary user

A Peruvian small-business owner, commercial lead, or bid coordinator who occasionally evaluates public-sector opportunities and does not have a dedicated procurement team.

## Core job

> When I receive a tender document, help me decide whether it is worth pursuing and show me what I must verify before investing more time.

## MVP promise

Upload one PDF and enter one company profile. Receive a cited report covering:

- basic tender facts;
- mandatory requirements;
- required documents;
- potential disqualifiers;
- company-fit assessment;
- go/no-go recommendation;
- next-step checklist.

## Non-goals

The MVP does not:

- submit bids;
- replace legal or procurement review;
- guarantee eligibility;
- scrape every SEACE opportunity;
- generate final legal declarations;
- support every LATAM country;
- store sensitive company files.

## Success metrics

The first usable milestone is reached when:

- 10 businesses have tested it;
- 50 real tenders have been analyzed;
- mandatory-requirement precision is at least 85%;
- at least 90% of displayed citations point to the correct page;
- median initial screening time falls below 10 minutes;
- at least 3 users make a real pursue / do-not-pursue decision with the report.

## Key product risks

1. Scanned PDFs may require OCR.
2. Procurement language can make mandatory and optional conditions difficult to distinguish.
3. Company profiles may omit evidence, requiring an UNCLEAR status rather than a positive match.
4. A persuasive-looking AI answer can create false confidence, so citations and warnings are mandatory.
